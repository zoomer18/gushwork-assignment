Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing
Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

Step 1/8: Raw Material

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution
Step 2/8: Extrusion

High-Temperature Extrusion Process

HDPE pellets are melted at precise temperatures and forced through a die to form continuous pipe with consistent wall thickness.

  • Temperature-controlled extruder zones
  • Continuous melt pressure monitoring
Step 3/8: Cooling

Controlled Cooling & Annealing

Extruded pipe passes through water cooling tanks to set dimensional properties and relieve internal stresses for long-term performance.

  • Multi-stage water cooling tanks
  • Stress-relief annealing process
Step 4/8: Sizing

Precision Vacuum Sizing

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • ±0.1mm dimensional accuracy
  • Real-time laser diameter gauging
Step 5/8: Quality Control

Rigorous Quality Control

Every pipe undergoes multi-stage inspection including hydrostatic pressure testing, dimensional verification, and surface quality checks.

  • Hydrostatic pressure test per IS 4984
  • Full dimensional compliance report
Step 6/8: Marking

Automated Pipe Marking

Each pipe is inkjet-marked with product specifications, batch number, pressure rating, and certification details for full traceability.

  • Inkjet marking at 3m intervals
  • Batch and certification data encoded
Step 7/8: Cutting

Precision Cutting & Finishing

CNC-controlled cutting systems deliver exact pipe lengths with clean, burr-free ends ready for immediate installation or further processing.

  • CNC-controlled cut-off saw
  • Automated deburring and chamfering
Step 8/8: Packaging

Quality Packaging & Dispatch

Pipes are bundled, wrapped, and palletised for safe transport. GPS-tracked logistics ensure on-time delivery across India.

  • Weather-resistant protective wrapping
  • GPS-tracked pan-India delivery
